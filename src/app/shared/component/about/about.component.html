About us

At Repox, we are deeply passionate about simplifying the intricate world of software development. Our journey began with a vision to provide developers with a powerful yet accessible monorepo management tool. As software engineers ourselves, we intimately understand the challenges that arise when managing multiple projects within a single Git repository.

We firmly believe that software development should be more straightforward and productive, and Repox has been purpose-built to deliver precisely that.
